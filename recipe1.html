Ingredients

  • 200g pasta
  • 1 cup tomato sauce
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Boil the pasta in salted water until al dente.
  2. Sauté garlic in olive oil until fragrant.
  3. Add tomato sauce and simmer for 5 minutes.
  4. Toss pasta with the sauce and garnish with Parmesan and basil.
  5. Serve hot and enjoy!

Nutritional Information

Approx. 400 calories per serving, with balanced carbohydrates, protein, and fats.
