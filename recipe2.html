Ingredients

  • Mixed greens (spinach, arugula, kale)
  • Cherry tomatoes, halved
  • Cucumber slices
  • Avocado chunks
  • Grilled chicken strips or tofu
  • Your favorite dressing

Instructions

  1. Wash and prep the salad greens and vegetables.
  2. Arrange the greens on a bowl and top with tomatoes, cucumber, avocado, and chicken or tofu.
  3. Drizzle your favorite dressing on top and toss gently.
  4. Serve immediately for the freshest taste.

Nutritional Information

Approx. 350 calories per serving with a healthy mix of fiber, vitamins, and lean protein.
